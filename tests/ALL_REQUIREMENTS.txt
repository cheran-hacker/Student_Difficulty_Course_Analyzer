=============================================
      STUDENT ANALYZER - MASTER REQUIREMENTS
=============================================

This file contains the consolidated requirements for all advanced modules for the "Master Application" upgrade.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE 1: AI PREDICTIVE ENGINE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Transform the app from "Descriptive Analytics" to "Predictive Analytics".

1. Workload Forecaster
   - Input: Historical feedback "Hours/Week", Syllabus topic count.
   - Output: A graph showing expected workload intensity week-by-week.
   - Prediction: "Expect a Heavy workload in Week 8 (Midterms)."

2. Difficulty Predictor
   - Algorithm: Compare Student GPA vs Course Difficulty Rating.
   - Output: Personal Difficulty Score (1-10).
   - Insight: "Based on your grades in Math 101, this course will be HARD (8/10)."

3. Sentiment Analysis
   - Tech: Natural Language Processing (NLP).
   - Function: Scan reviews for keywords (Stress, Easy, Boring, Inspiring).
   - Output: "70% of students feel 'Inspired' by this course."

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE 2: GAMIFICATION & REWARDS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Incentivize students to provide high-quality feedback.

1. XP (Experience Points) System
   - +10 XP: Rate a course.
   - +50 XP: Write a detailed review (>50 words).
   - +5 XP: Daily Login.

2. Levels & Titles
   - Level 1 (0 XP): "Fresher"
   - Level 5 (1000 XP): "Scholar"
   - Level 10 (5000 XP): "Dean's List"

3. Badges
   - "Early Bird": First to review in a semester.
   - "Helper": Your review got 10 upvotes.
   - "Critic": Wrote 10 detailed reviews.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE 3: SOCIAL LEARNING HUB
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Create a community around courses.

1. Real-Time Chat (Socket.io)
   - Live chat room for every active course.
   - "Who's Online" counter.

2. Study Buddy Finder
   - Status Toggle: "Looking for Group".
   - Filters: "Library", "Cafe", "Remote".
   - Privacy: Only show to verified students in the same course.

3. Review Upvoting
   - Students can mark reviews as "Helpful".
   - Most helpful reviews appear at the top.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MODULE 4: ENTERPRISE SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goal: Secure the platform for 10,000+ users.

1. RBAC 2.0 (Role-Based Access Control)
   - Admin: Full Access.
   - Moderator: Can delete comments, cannot delete courses.
   - Student: Read/Write own data only.

2. Audit Logs
   - Record every admin action: "Admin X deleted Course Y at 10:00 AM".
   - Immutable record for compliance.

3. Anti-Bot / Anti-Spam
   - Rate limiting: Max 2 reviews per hour.
   - Honeypot fields in registration form.

=============================================
END OF REQUIREMENTS
